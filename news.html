News & Notices

Official announcements, press releases, and public consultations from government agencies of the Republic.

14Jul 2026
NoticeElectoral Commission of Rhodesia

Electoral Commission Opens Roll Ahead of General Election

Constituency registration centres open nationwide today as the Commission prepares the voters’ roll for the coming general election. Citizens may confirm or update their registration at any District Registry office or online through the National ID service. The roll closes for updates thirty days before polling.

02Jul 2026
Press ReleaseNational Archives of Rhodesia

National Archives Completes Digitisation of Settlement Records

The Office of the Chief Archivist announces that the full 1966 Salisbury Agreement archive, including negotiation minutes and the original Settlement instrument, is now available for public research at the National Archives reading room in Salisbury and, for the first time, as certified digital reproductions on request.

19Jun 2026
ConsultationMinistry of Lands

Ministry of Lands Opens Public Consultation on Registry Modernisation

Landholders and industry bodies are invited to submit written comments on proposed changes to title registration procedures, intended to move the Land Registry to a fully digital cadastral system. The consultation closes on 30 August; submissions may be made through any District Land Office.

01Jun 2026
Press ReleaseOffice of the President

Government Announces Programme for the Diamond Jubilee of the Settlement

The Office of the President has published the official programme of commemorations marking the sixtieth anniversary of the Settlement of 1966, including a joint sitting of Parliament, a public exhibition at the National Archives, and community events in every province.

22May 2026
NoticeMinistry of Home Affairs

Notice on Processing Times for Residency Applications

Owing to continued growth in applications from the region, the Ministry advises that standard residency-permit processing times are temporarily extended to twelve weeks. Applicants may check status online using their reference number.

03May 2026
NoticeRhodesian Police Service

Rhodesian Police Service Opens Annual Recruitment Round

Applications are now open for the Service’s annual constable and officer-cadet intake. Eligibility, physical-standards information, and application forms are available through the Ministry of Justice.
