Agency Directory

Ministries, departments, and independent commissions of the Government of the Republic of Rhodesia, with references to their constitutional basis.

Office of the President & Government House

The Presidency, the Republic’s Head of State under Chapter III of the Constitution, headquartered at Government House, Salisbury.

Constitution, Article 8 →
Est. 1966

Parliament of Rhodesia

The bicameral legislature, comprising the Senate and the House of Assembly, responsible for enacting the laws of the Republic.

Constitution, Article 4 →
Est. 1966

Office of the Prime Minister & Cabinet

The Prime Minister, drawn from and accountable to the House of Assembly, leads the Cabinet and the day-to-day administration of government.

Constitution, Article 10 →
Est. 1966

Supreme Court of Rhodesia

The highest court of the Republic, exercising final appellate jurisdiction under Roman-Dutch common law.

Judicial Service Commission

Independent body responsible for the appointment, discipline, and tenure of judicial officers.

Ministry of Justice

Oversees the administration of justice, prosecutorial services, and policy responsibility for the Rhodesian Police Service.

Rhodesian Police Service

The Republic’s civil police force, reconstituted from the former British South Africa Police at independence.
